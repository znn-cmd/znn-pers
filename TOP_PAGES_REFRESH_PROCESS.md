# Top Pages Refresh Process

This process keeps high-performing pages fresh for SEO and GEO.

## Scope
- Top 20 landing pages by organic traffic
- Top 20 pages by impressions and declining CTR
- Core pillar pages in `articles`, `roasts`, `analytics`

## Cadence
- Weekly: metrics review
- Every 30 days: refresh 5-8 pages
- Every 90 days: full review of all pillar pages

## Refresh Checklist
1. Validate that title and excerpt still match search intent.
2. Update publication context:
   - keep `datePublished` original
   - refresh `dateModified` by updating content
3. Improve TL;DR and Key Takeaways clarity.
4. Add or update FAQ based on real user questions.
5. Refresh Sources with newer references.
6. Add 2-4 internal links to newer relevant materials.
7. Remove weak or absolute claims.

## Acceptance Criteria
- Page has all mandatory sections:
  - TL;DR
  - FAQ
  - Key Takeaways
  - Sources
- `dateModified` changed after meaningful update
- At least 2 new internal links
- No unverifiable absolute statements
